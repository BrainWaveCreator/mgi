FAQ

Common Questions

How quickly can I get coverage?

In many cases, we can bind coverage the same day. For standard policies, most quotes are ready within 15 minutes to a few hours depending on complexity.

What types of businesses do you work with?

We work with contractors, small businesses, property owners, and individuals across Florida. From general contractors to restaurants to rental property owners, we've got you covered.

Do you offer premium financing?

Yes! We offer premium financing options to help you manage cash flow. Instead of paying your annual premium upfront, you can spread payments out over time. Ask us for details.

Do you serve all of Florida?

Yes. We're licensed statewide and work with clients from Miami to Jacksonville, Tampa to the Keys. If you're in Florida, we can help.

Can I bundle multiple policies?

Absolutely. Bundling policies like general liability, workers' comp, and commercial auto can save you money and simplify your coverage. We'll help you find the right combination.

What if I need a certificate of insurance (COI) fast?

We understand deadlines. Once your policy is bound, we can typically issue certificates the same day. Need it for a job starting tomorrow? Call us.
