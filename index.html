FAQ

Questions? We've Got Answers.

How fast can I get coverage?

Most policies can be bound the same day we receive your information. If you need a certificate of insurance for a job tomorrow, we can usually make that happen.

Do I need a contractor's license?

Not for all policies. We work with licensed and unlicensed contractors. Tell us your situation and we'll find the right coverage.

What if I'm not sure what coverage I need?

That's what we're here for. Call us and we'll walk through your business and figure out exactly what you need. No pressure, no obligation.

How is this different from my current broker?

Lots of brokers can get you a policy. What's different here is what happens after. You talk to the same person who wrote your policy when something changes on your job. We shop your renewal instead of letting it auto-renew at a higher rate. And we'll tell you when you're over-insured, not just when you need more coverage. Try us on a quote and see if the conversation feels different.
